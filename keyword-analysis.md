## Top 5 Priorities

1. **Fix all-caps product titles** - `period-starter-bundle-5-pack`, `tpp-combo`, dance and swim edition bundles. All-caps CTA copy in image titles signals the same issue likely exists in the Shopify product title field.
2. **Lead product titles with keywords, not brand names** - `period-underwear-girls` (Bumpers Briefs), `underliner-girls-leak-proof` (UnderLiner), `underaustin` (UnderAustin), `stain-slayer` all bury the keyword.
3. **Resolve the keyword cannibalization** between `/products/period-swim-undies` and `/products/g-fit-underswim-period-swim-underwear` - both target "period swim underwear".
4. **Switch `/collections/period-starter-packs` primary keyword to "first period kit"** - higher transactional intent from the parent buying audience.
5. **Noindex utility and dynamic collection pages** - `ex-bundles`, `pre-orders`, `best-selling-products`, `newest-products`, `all`, `us-shop-all` dilute crawl budget.
